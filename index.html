About

Who am I?

Hello everyone! I’m Cathy, a yoga teacher based in and around London.

Why yoga?

I come from a creative background and discovered yoga whilst studying at Architecture school. It was really a place for me to let go, clear the mind and work my body.Throughout this time, I became inspired to give back everything that yoga had given me. I wanted to help others discover yoga and benefit from it like I did. Since graduating I have trained to become a yoga teacher with Yoga Alliance and have enjoyed teaching across London ever since.

This unplanned journey into yoga has developed into being a huge part of my life. I never thought I would make it as a yoga teacher, but becoming one has been one of the best things I’ve done. I hope to share the yoga love with everyone and anyone I can!

What's your style?

I’ve been fortunate enough to have had exposure and inspiration from a variety of yoga teachers and styles. My main passion and forte lies in Dynamic Vinyasa Flow, but I also teach a range of styles, including Ashtanga, Power and Restorative/Hatha yoga.

What are your classes like?

You will probably float out of my classes beaming with enlightenment! Jokes aside though, my classes are full of fun, energy and upbeat vibes – guaranteed to leave you on a yogic high. I love to encourage all levels of practitioners to my classes and promise to give all my students a good challenge!

Private Classes

Practice at your own pace with your very own made-to-measure private Yoga classes, great for all levels from the new practitioner to the advanced Yogi. We’ll be working together to deepen your practice and work towards achieving that personal intention of yours. Whether it is to benefit from 1-2-1 guidance, or even to focus on recovering from an injury, private Yoga classes will always offer that something special to you.

Class styles offered in Dynamic Vinyasa Flow, Power, or Restorative/Hatha Yoga. Equipment can be provided free of charge.

Group Classes

Are you a keen Yogi couple or a group of up to 4 friends? Great! Benefit from smaller Yoga class environments with the people close to your heart. Tailor made practice brought straight to your door or a location of choice.
